About Asthma

Asthma is a chronic condition that affects the airways, causing difficulty in breathing. In emergencies, such as an asthma attack, quick action is essential.

Emergency Instructions

  1. Help the patient sit upright and remain calm.
  2. Assist the patient in using their inhaler if available.
  3. Call emergency services if the attack is severe.
  4. Monitor the patient's breathing until help arrives.
