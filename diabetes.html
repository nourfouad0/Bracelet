About Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose). In emergencies, such as hypoglycemia (low blood sugar), quick action is essential.

Emergency Instructions

  1. Check if the patient is conscious.
  2. If unconscious, call emergency services immediately.
  3. Provide sugar (e.g., juice or candy) if the patient is conscious and able to swallow.
  4. Monitor the patient until help arrives.
