About the Project

Our bracelet system is designed to provide instant assistance to chronic patients in emergencies. Each bracelet features a unique QR code that, when scanned, displays critical information and step-by-step instructions to help bystanders provide immediate care.

How It Works

  1. Patient wears a colored bracelet.
  2. In an emergency, someone scans the QR code.
  3. The QR code displays critical information (e.g., medical condition, emergency contacts, first-aid instructions).
  4. A short video or text guide helps the responder provide immediate assistance.
