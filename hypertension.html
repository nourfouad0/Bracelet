About Hypertension

Hypertension, or high blood pressure, is a condition where the force of blood against the artery walls is too high. In emergencies, such as a hypertensive crisis, immediate action is required.

Emergency Instructions

  1. Help the patient sit down and remain calm.
  2. Call emergency services immediately.
  3. Monitor the patient's breathing and pulse.
  4. Do not give the patient any medication unless instructed by a medical professional.
