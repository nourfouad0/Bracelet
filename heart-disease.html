About Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the heart, including coronary artery disease and heart attacks. In emergencies, such as a heart attack, quick action can save lives.

Emergency Instructions

  1. Call emergency services immediately.
  2. Help the patient sit down and remain calm.
  3. If the patient is unconscious, perform CPR if trained.
  4. Do not give the patient anything to eat or drink.
